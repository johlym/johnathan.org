Watch what you're doing

Don't be like me and double-publish issues of The Minimalist. If you're new to the community, you won't know what I'm talking about. In short, watch what you're clicking. In honor of such mistakes, I'm taking it to the PUN level. We're looking at an entire list of watches... fine time pieces if you will... with one common theme: minimalistic faces.
