ARCHITECTURE

The Dell

From Elliott Architects Ltd., The Dell is a 4-bedroom home and its property is neatly organized into four sections with purposes for each space. The first floor exterior is clad with slate while the second is vertically-oriented timber. Large windows and doors on the lower level give way to wonderful amounts of light, even with the dense forest coming right up to the edge of the property.

elliottarchitects.co.uk

PRODUCTS

LG Signature OLED TV W

If you've ever stared at your TV and thought, "you know, I need to get crazy," this is your display. At 65" and 77", the display itself is detached from the base, where the components lie. This leads to an insanely thin television (less than 1/4" !) featuring HDR with Dolby Vision, Dolby Atmos, 4 HDMI, 802.11ac Wi-Fi, and a price tag of under $20,000 USD.

I'll take two.

lg.com

Henry Floor Lamp

From Kylle, this beautifully unique standing lamp features a quad-pod walnut leg arrangement, a brass cross bar keeping things stable, and a cord that's completely hidden in one of the legs. At 67" tall and 16" wide at the base, this is one fresh piece.

kyllesebree.com

TruLine Plaster-In LED System

Let's face it. Regular light fixtures are so 19th century. It's time we step up our lighting game. Lightology has done just that with there Truline Plaster-In LED system.

What does that mean? it means your lights are in your walls and or ceilings. Flush and full of crazy clean vibes. They're not cheap, though, a 12" kit starts at around $280 USD and a full blown build can reach $10K. The price we pay for awesome, I suppose.

lightology.com

Ace Floor Lamp

Someone, somewhere is longing for a stick for a lamp. I don't blame them. I'm all for simple and clean. This'll take it to the extreme so be ready for it. I can actually see this being very useful in large open spaces.

gagan-design.com

Dioscuri Table Lamp

lately I've been quite impressed with the look of the orb-style table lamp. These from YLighting come in four sizes, 6", 10", 14", and 17" and look absolutely fantastic. The next time I decorate a living space, I'll have to give these a go.

ylighting.com
